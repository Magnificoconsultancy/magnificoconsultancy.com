About Us

A modern consultancy built around planning discipline, delivery control, and project confidence.

Magnifico Consultancy is positioned to support owners, contractors, subcontractors, and project teams with a clear, structured, and performance-driven approach to project execution. Our objective is not only to produce schedules and reports, but to create a planning system that helps decision-makers anticipate risks, coordinate stakeholders, and maintain momentum from award through completion.

We combine engineering understanding with hands-on project planning methodology to develop realistic programmes, monitor progress, identify slippage early, and recommend practical recovery strategies. Our services are designed to add value across the lifecycle of a project, from preconstruction planning and tender support to execution monitoring, extension-of-time assessment, and management reporting.

With a strong focus on the UAE construction market and a service model that can extend across multiple sectors, Magnifico Consultancy aims to be a dependable partner for organizations seeking technical clarity, stronger schedule control, and a more professional project delivery framework.

Services

Comprehensive consultancy support across planning, controls, and project delivery.

Our service offering is designed to meet the practical needs of construction and engineering projects at different stages of the project lifecycle. Whether the requirement is baseline planning, recovery planning, management reporting, progress assessment, or delay review, our focus remains the same: clarity, structure, and delivery value.

Project Planning

Baseline Scheduling & Programme Development

We prepare comprehensive project programmes aligned with contract scope, procurement strategy, sequencing logic, and construction methodology. Our schedules are developed to support both management visibility and site-level execution.

Project Controls

Progress Monitoring & Performance Reporting

We establish reporting structures that track planned versus actual progress, identify slippage trends, and support management with concise, actionable performance updates tailored to project priorities.

Construction Management

Look-Ahead Planning & Execution Coordination

We support short-term planning and coordination by translating master schedules into practical look-ahead plans that improve trade interface control, site readiness, and resource focus.

Delay Analysis

EOT Support & Schedule Forensics

We review delay events, programme impact, and time-related entitlement issues through structured schedule analysis that supports notices, narratives, claim preparation, and negotiation.

Advisory

Tender Planning & Bid Programme Preparation

We assist during pre-award phases by preparing tender programmes, methodology narratives, milestone strategies, and technical planning inputs that strengthen submissions and commercial positioning.

Digital Insight

Dashboards, Presentations & Decision Support

We convert raw planning and progress data into concise executive dashboards and visual reports that help leadership teams understand status, priorities, risks, and next actions.
